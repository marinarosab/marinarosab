# Caixa Geral de Depósitos

- Type: Professional Experience
- Role: Scrum Master
- Period: February 2025 - July 2025
- Context: Via NTT Data - Lisbon, Portugal

## Context

Caixa Geral de Depósitos is Portugal's largest state-owned bank, operating across retail, business and institutional banking. As a Scrum Master embedded via NTT DATA, I supported two squads working on distinct product and operational domains - each with its own ways of working, tooling, stakeholder landscape, and delivery challenges.

The environment was complex by nature: large organisation, multiple interdependencies, regulated sector, and a technology landscape that combined modern delivery practices with legacy processes and tooling. My role was to help each team work with more clarity, structure and visibility - without imposing a one-size-fits-all approach.
