# 🇵🇹 Community Digital Initiatives

Beyond facilitation and delivery, I've been actively involved in the Portuguese agile community, contributing to the kind of spaces where knowledge is shared, people connect, and the ecosystem grows.

That involvement goes beyond attending events. It includes helping organise them, shaping how they communicate, and taking ownership of initiatives end-to-end, from identifying a need to making something real out of it.

## 🗓️ Regional Scrum Gathering Lisbon 2026
- Status: Live
- Website: https://www.rsglisbon.com/

### About the event
The Regional Scrum Gathering (RSG) is a global initiative by the Scrum Alliance, bringing together agile practitioners for a day of talks, workshops and community connection. In 2026, TugÁgil organised the Lisbon edition for the second consecutive year, and this was also my second time as part of the organising team.

### My involvement
Being part of the organising team meant contributing across multiple fronts: event strategy, ticketing, sponsorship outreach, and marketing. There was no single owner per area; everyone contributed where needed, and the work was collective.

Within that broader involvement, my concrete ownership was the event website: the digital face of the RSG Lisbon 2026.

### What I did on the website
- Identified the need for a centralised, clear event communication structure.
- Defined the content architecture: what information needed to exist, in what order, and for what audience.
- Designed the user flow and information hierarchy, from landing to registration.
- Built and published the front-end using GitHub and Vercel.
- Iterated with the team on content, structure and continuous improvements.

### What this actually was
Not a website project. A communication and coordination challenge: how do we make sure that anyone who hears about this event can find everything they need in one place, understand what it is, and take action? The front-end was the implementation tool. The thinking behind it was product and content strategy.

## 🎯 TugÁgil Experience 2026
- Status: In progress · Event planned for October 2026.

### About the event
TugÁgil Experience 2026 is a TugÁgil new initiative born from two years of organising the RSG Lisbon and from something that became clear along the way: there was appetite for more. More connection, more sharing, more space for professionals and organisations in the Portuguese agile, innovation and technology ecosystem to meet, learn and grow together.

TugÁgil Experience is a community-native initiative, built from the inside out by the people who have been doing this work in Portugal.

### My involvement
As part of the broader organising team, contributing across different fronts. And again, my concrete ownership is the event's digital presence.

### What I'm doing on the website
- Structuring the GitHub repository and deployment pipeline from scratch.
- Defining the content strategy: what the site needs to communicate, to whom, and in what sequence.
- Designing the information architecture and user experience.
- Building iteratively, starting with what needs to exist at launch, with room to evolve as the event takes shape.

This initiative is recently yet.
The work is underway.

## What these initiatives say about how I work
I don't wait for the right conditions to appear before something gets started. If a need is clear, I engage with it. If something needs to be built, I learn what I need to learn and build it. If it needs to be tested and published, I do that too.

This is not about being a developer. It is about ownership: the willingness to move from identified need to working solution, making decisions along the way, and not letting a skill gap become a reason to stop.

That way of thinking shows up in delivery work, and in facilitation as well.
